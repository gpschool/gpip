By Rail

The nearest railway station is Bletchley Railway Station in Milton Keynes. Bletchley Park is 300 yards walk from the railway station.

There is a fast efficient service to Bletchley Railway Station from many stations in the U.K.. Check the national rail enquiries site for details.

By Air

Birmingham

There is a direct train to Bletchley Park from Birmingham International Airport, it takes about an hour.

Other Airports

It is also possible to take the train from other U.K. airports (Heathrow, Gatwick, Manchester, Luton, Stansted). However, in each case the journey is over two hours.
